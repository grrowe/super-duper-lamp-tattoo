The Collective

Masters of the Everlasting Mark.

Our residents are more than tattooists; they are curators of skin, specializing in precise editorial aesthetics and high-contrast narratives.

01
Specialty: Dark Illustrative

Elias Thorne

With a decade of experience in the shadows, Elias specializes in high-contrast illustrative pieces that blend Victorian gothic elements with modern anatomical precision. His work is characterized by deep blacks and ethereal line work.

02
Specialty: Fine Line Realism

Selene Vane

Selene is the master of the "Whisper Needle." Her work focuses on micro-realism and fine line botanicals that feel like they were etched into the skin by sunlight itself. Elegant, timeless, and hauntingly beautiful.

03
Specialty: Neo-Traditional Noir

Marcus Draven

Marcus reimagines traditional motifs through a dark, editorial lens. His "Noir" style utilizes heavy saturated blacks paired with stark skin breaks to create bold, readable art that commands attention from across the room.
